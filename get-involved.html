Membership

What you get as a member

  • Take part in any of our six activities
  • A trained guide or buddy whenever you need one
  • All adapted equipment provided — just turn up
  • Invitations to every social and event
  • A warm, judgement-free community from day one
  • Help with travel and access so getting there is easy

Volunteer roles

Ways to lend a hand

Sighted guide

Walk, run or play alongside a member, helping them move and take part with confidence. The most in-demand role — and hugely rewarding.

Session helper

Set up equipment, welcome newcomers and keep sessions running smoothly. Perfect if you have a few hours to spare.

Coach or assistant

Share your sporting know-how. We will support you to adapt your coaching for visually impaired players.

Driver

Help members get to and from sessions. A lift can be the difference between taking part and missing out.
