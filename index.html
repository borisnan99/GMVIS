Who we are

A place to belong, on and off the pitch

Greater Manchester Vis was founded on a simple belief: that sight loss should never be a barrier to success — in sport, in friendship or in life. From that idea grew a thriving group offering coached sessions, relaxed socials and a warm welcome to everyone.

Whether you've played sport all your life or have never tried before, our guides and volunteers make sure you can take part with confidence. Sighted family, friends and supporters are always welcome too.

What we stand for

Everything we do is built on three promises

Welcoming

No pressure, no judgement — everyone is welcome. From competitive sport to a gentle walk and a cuppa, we'll find a way for you to take part.

Inclusive by Design

Every session is adapted for people with sight loss, with trained guides, audible equipment and supportive coaching.

Community First

More than sport — a place to make friends, build confidence and belong.

Powered by people

None of this happens without our volunteers

Every guide, every lift, every welcome at the door is given freely by people who simply want others to share what sport gave them. To our selfless volunteers — thank you. You are the heart of this club.
